🔍 Model Explainability — How GigShield Prices Fairly
What Makes Premiums Go Down ↓
−
Low-flood zone history: Workers in zones with <2 flood events in 5 years save ₹5–₹12/week
−
Worker tenure: 3+ year partners earn a ₹4 trust discount (lower claim fraud probability)
−
Dry season weeks: October–December premiums automatically drop 15–20% in most zones
−
Clean claim history: No inflated premiums for honest claimers — claim history only affects fraud score
What Makes Premiums Go Up ↑
+
Active monsoon forecast: Rain probability >60% in next 7 days adds ₹8–₹18
+
High AQI zones: Workers in Delhi/NCR during winter AQI spikes pay a ₹5–₹10 pollution premium
+
Extreme heat forecast: Summer weeks with 45°C+ predicted adds ₹5
+
Platform instability: If platform has had 2+ outages recently, outage premium applies
🤖 Model architecture: XGBoost regressor trained on 18 months of synthetic gig-worker disruption data + OpenMeteo historical weather. Features normalized via StandardScaler. Weekly refit every Sunday 00:00 IST using latest 7-day weather forecast as input.
