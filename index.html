Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing

500 delivery partners. Fake GPS. Coordinated fraud. GigShield's multi-layer defense catches the ring — not the worker.

🎯 How We Detect GPS Spoofing

  • GPS coordinates cross-validated against cell tower triangulation and IP geolocation — all three must agree within 200m
  • Speed-impossible jumps: if location changes faster than any vehicle can travel, it's flagged immediately
  • Altitude & signal metadata analysis — spoofed GPS typically lacks real elevation noise and satellite count variation
  • Historical movement pattern baseline — sudden deviation from a worker's usual delivery radius triggers review

🔍 Weather Correlation Checks

  • Claimed location weather must match data from at least 2 independent weather APIs — single-source manipulation is caught
  • Hyperlocal radar data (2km grid) compared against claimed GPS zone — zone boundary fraud is flagged
  • Time-stamp validation: weather trigger must have been active during the claimed loss window, not before or after
  • Historical weather consistency — pattern analysis of past claims vs. historical events for the same area

🕸️ Ring Pattern Detection

  • Graph analysis of claim clusters — if 50+ accounts claim the same trigger in the same micro-zone within minutes, ring probability score activates
  • Device fingerprinting: multiple accounts from the same device or shared IP subnet are automatically escalated
  • Account age & onboarding velocity tracking — mass registrations within 48 hours of a weather event are high-risk scored
  • Social graph: shared bank accounts, UPI handles, or phone numbers across claiming accounts are cross-referenced

✅ Protecting Honest Workers

  • Tiered response: first anomaly → silent flag only; second → soft review; third → manual verification. No instant rejection.
  • Legitimate stranded workers retain genuine environmental data (IMD official alerts, platform order-pause status) that fakers can't replicate
  • Appeals process: all fraud-flagged claims go to a human review queue with 4-hour SLA — honest workers are made whole
  • Behavioral trust score accretes over time — long-standing workers with clean history get the benefit of the doubt on edge cases
